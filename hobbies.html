Hobbies and Interests

Both myself and my wife are very much outdoor people and experienced mountaineers. I have climbed mountains in California, the UK and Mount Kenya in Kenya. Together we have climbed a number of Munros during our time in Scotland. I am a keen writer and have drafted my first novel. I play guitar and sing, perform in local pubs and clubs at open mic and jam nights with popular songs as well as some that I have written myself. I am also keen to get back into sports that I enjoyed in the army such as skiing, SCUBA diving and skydiving when weather and time permit.
