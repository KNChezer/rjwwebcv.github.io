About Me

I am an ex-British soldier and member of the Corps of Royal Engineers. I am now living in California with my wife and son. Over the course of my career, I have always strived towards continuous personal and professional development, and it is a quality that I have taken with me into civilian life. Constantly adapting to new environments and relishing a challenge, I am never satisfied with the knowledge that something works. I always try to figure out the ‘how’ and the ‘why’. I have experience in many different areas, being either a team member or team leader and working independently. From climbing mountains and leaping from aircraft to fixing broken equipment and heavy machinery in some of the most austere environments in the world, leading and managing teams, and even building websites, there is no challenge too big and no amount that is ‘too much’ to learn. If I were working for you, I wouldn’t be satisfied with just being able to do my job. I’d want to be good enough to teach it.
