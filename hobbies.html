Hobbies and Interests

Both my wife and I are very much outdoor people and experienced mountaineers. I have climbed mountains in California, the UK and Africa. Together, we have climbed several Munros during our time in Scotland. I am a keen writer, having published multiple short works of fiction and non-fiction for many clients as a freelancer, as well as my own novels. I play guitar, sing, and perform in local pubs and clubs at open mic and jam nights with popular songs and originals written by myself. I am also a licensed BSAC SCUBA diver, BPA Skydiver, and avid rock climber.
